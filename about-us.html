About Us

Lakshya Manwani

Weaving Dreams into Frames: A Cinematic Saga with Safarnama Films

Lakshya Manwani, the visionary behind Safarnama Films, has redefined Indian wedding cinematography with creativity and innovation. With a deep understanding of wedding traditions and emotions, Lakshya and his team capture the essence of every celebration, preserving memories that last a lifetime.
Based in Delhi, Safarnama Films has expanded its reach to top wedding destinations like Dubai and Jaipur. Their reputation for excellence, especially in vibrant Punjabi weddings, has made them a sought-after name in the industry.
Lakshya’s passion extends beyond filmmaking, with ventures like Sindhiname, a cloud kitchen for authentic Sindhi cuisine, and Labyrama, a platform for maternity and newborn photography. His commitment to quality over quantity ensures every project is a masterpiece, making Safarnama Films a leader in capturing love stories.
